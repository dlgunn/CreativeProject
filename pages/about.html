About

What is Storywriter?

Storywriter is a collaborative story-writing project. Anyone can start a new story or add to existing stories. Users can also upvote additions they enjoy or downvote those they don't. Users whose stories are upvoted gain reputation, allowing them to access more perks and benefits, even donations from fans.

Since each version of the story is kept, you have the freedom to explore every version of your favorite stories. Still feel like something is missing? Write your own twist in the plot!

Storywriter is currently working on implementing even more features! Stay tuned for scene by scene composition and snowflake method integration!
